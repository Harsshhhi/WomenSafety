How to Use Here Am

1. Sign Up or Log In

Create an account using your phone number or log in to access all features of Here Am.

2. Set Up Your Profile

Add your emergency contacts and personal details to ensure quick access during emergencies.

3. Explore Safest Path

Use the Safest Path feature to find the most secure routes to your destination.

4. Add Emergency Contacts

Save emergency numbers like police, ambulance, and trusted contacts for quick access.

5. Share Live Location

Share your real-time location with trusted contacts to ensure they know where you are.

6. Use SOS Feature

In an emergency, press the SOS button to send an alert to your contacts with your location.

7. Chat with AI Chatbot

Get personalized safety advice and tips from our AI chatbot anytime.

8. Join the Community

Share your experiences and read others' stories to stay informed and connected.
